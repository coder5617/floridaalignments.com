Our Story

At Florida Alignment & Suspension, we have been providing quality automotive repair and maintenance services to the Orlando area for over 20 years. Our experienced technicians are dedicated to keeping your vehicle running safely and efficiently.

We specialize in alignment and suspension work, but our expertise extends to all aspects of automotive repair and maintenance. From routine oil changes to complex brake repairs, we have the skills and equipment to handle any job.
